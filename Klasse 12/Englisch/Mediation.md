# Mediation

## Preparing

- Text title, type, purpose
- General information
- Mark text relevant keyword
- Leave out unessery information

## Writing

- Suitable language
- Mention source
- Sum up relevant points
- Paraphrase/Explain if you do not know the exact translation

## Checking

- Check for false friends
- Incorrect tense
- Spelling
- Prepositions
- Sound natural
